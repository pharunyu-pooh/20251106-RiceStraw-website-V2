Our Vision

"To transform sustainability into real opportunities for Thai farmers." We're committed to strengthening Thailand's rice industry. By supplying our products to restaurants and hotels, we direct economic opportunities for farmers. This isn't just about business; it's about building a sustainable future by investing in the people who cultivate our land.

Why Change To CHAONA Rice Straw?

Why? Because they aren't just an eco-friendly product, they're a product that cares for Thai farmers!

100% Biodegradable —It's Rice

CHAONA Rice Straw are made from locally grown rice, they dissolve back into nature effortlessly. Within 90 days. No trace, no guilt. Also edible, if you don't want to wait—

Setting New Standards

Sustainability is now industry standard. With CHAONA, your straw reflects your dedication beyond sustainability. The industry benchmark begins here.
